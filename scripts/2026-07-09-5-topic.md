# Farm Animals Wake Up Song

## Topic
- Hook: Benny rings a farm bell and sings animal sounds at sunrise
- Age: 2-8
- Category: animals
- Moral: Listening closely helps everyone join the song
- Visual: Red barn, sunrise, cow, horse, duck, sheep, Benny with a little bell

## Script
DING DING! Benny rings the FARM BELL loud!
The cow says moo to the morning cloud.
The horse goes neigh by the red barn door.
The duck says quack, then waddles some more.
The sheep says baa in a fluffy row.
Animal sounds help little ears grow.
DING DING! Sing good morning, farm friends!
