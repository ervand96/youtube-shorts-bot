# Moon Puppy Bedtime Story for Kids

## Topic
- Hook: A tiny moon puppy tiptoes through cloud pillows before bedtime
- Age: 2-8
- Category: bedtime
- Moral: Gentle breaths and cozy routines help little ones rest
- Visual: Moon puppy, soft clouds, blue blanket, twinkly bedroom stars

## Script
WOOF WHOOSH! The moon puppy tiptoes across fluffy clouds!
Benny tucks a blue blanket under sleepy paws.
One little yawn floats up to the stars.
Breathe in slow, then breathe out soft.
The moon puppy curls in a cloud pillow loft.
Benny whispers, you are safe, you are loved.
Tiny stars blink gently above.
WOOF WHOOSH! Sweet dreams, little friends, good night.
