# Farm Animals Song for Toddlers

## Topic
- Hook: Benny rides a tiny tractor and sings animal sounds around the farm
- Age: 2-8
- Category: animals
- Moral: Listening carefully helps us learn each farm animal sound
- Visual: Red tractor, barn, cow, duck, sheep, horse, sunny field

## Script
VROOM MOO! Benny's tractor rolls by the barn!
The cow says moo with a happy tail swing.
The duck says quack by the pond in spring.
The sheep says baa in a fluffy white coat.
The horse says neigh with a clip-clop note.
Listen, little farmers, what do you hear?
Animal sounds are loud and clear.
VROOM MOO! Sing farm sounds with Benny today!
