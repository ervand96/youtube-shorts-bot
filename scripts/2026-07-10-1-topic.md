# Wheels on the Bus Song for Toddlers

## Topic
- Hook: Benny drives a cheerful yellow bus through town with honks, doors, and wipers
- Age: 2-8
- Category: nursery
- Moral: Safe riding and friendly waves make every trip happy
- Visual: Yellow bus, Benny driver hat, waving kids, town road, splashy puddles

## Script
BEEP BEEP! The bus goes ROUND and ROUND!
The doors go open, then shut with a whoosh.
The wipers swish-swish through a shiny blue splash.
Benny says, buckle up, friends, nice and fast.
The horn says beep as we wave hello.
Round the town, nice and slow.
Sing with Benny, safe and bright.
BEEP BEEP! The bus rolls by with happy light!
