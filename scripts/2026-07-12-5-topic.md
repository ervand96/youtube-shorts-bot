# Farm Animals Morning Song

## Topic
- Hook: Benny wakes up with cock-a-doodle-do and sings animal sounds around the barn
- Age: 2-8
- Category: animals
- Moral: Every farm friend has a special sound to share
- Visual: Benny, red barn, rooster, cow, duck, sheep, sunrise hay field

## Script
COCK-A-DOODLE-DOO! The rooster wakes the farm!
Benny waves with a happy arm.
The cow says moo by the red barn door.
The duck says quack and waddles more.
The sheep says baa in the morning sun.
Sing each sound, one by one.
COCK-A-DOODLE-DOO! Farm friends sing good morning!
