# Learn Colors with Fruit for Toddlers

## Topic
- Hook: Benny taps bright fruit baskets and names colors one by one
- Age: 2-8
- Category: learning
- Moral: Trying one color at a time makes learning feel easy
- Visual: Benny, red apple, yellow banana, blue bowl, green pear, color cards

## Script
TAP TAP! Benny points to RED and shouts HOORAY!
Red apple, red apple, roll this way.
Yellow banana bends with a smile.
Blue bowl catches berries in a pile.
Green pear hops, one, two, three.
Colors are everywhere Benny can see.
TAP TAP! Say each color loud with me!
