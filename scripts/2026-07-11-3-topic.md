# Learn Colors with Toy Trains

## Topic
- Hook: Benny's toy train pops red, blue, yellow, and green cars
- Age: 2-8
- Category: learning
- Moral: Naming colors can be a fun team game
- Visual: Toy train track, colorful train cars, paint bubbles, smiling sun

## Script
POP POP! The color train rolls into town!
Red car first with an apple crown.
Blue car next like the sky so high.
Yellow car shines as it chugs by.
Green car wiggles like grass at play.
Benny names each color the happy way.
POP POP! Which bright color will you say?
