# Farm Animal Wake Up Song

## Topic
- Hook: Benny hears moo-moo and quack-quack at sunrise on the farm
- Age: 2-8
- Category: animals
- Moral: Every animal voice is special
- Visual: Sunny barn, cow, duck, sheep, chick, hay bales, dancing boots

## Script
MOO MOO! Benny wakes up on the sunny farm!
The cow says moo by the red barn door.
The duck says quack on the puddle floor.
The sheep says baa with a fluffy hop.
The little chick peeps and does not stop.
Benny claps for every animal sound.
MOO MOO! Happy farm friends dance around!
