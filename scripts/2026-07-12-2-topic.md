# Moon Pillow Bedtime Story for Kids

## Topic
- Hook: Benny hears a hush-whoosh moonbeam and helps a tiny pillow find sleepy sparkle
- Age: 2-8
- Category: bedtime
- Moral: Gentle breathing and kindness help little worries rest
- Visual: Benny in pajamas, crescent moon, soft pillow cloud, twinkle stars, cozy bedroom

## Script
HUSH WHOOSH! A moonbeam taps Benny's pillow!
Benny whispers, hello little glow.
The pillow feels wiggly and cannot sleep.
Benny counts stars in a voice so deep.
One soft breath, two sleepy eyes.
Three tiny dreams float through the skies.
HUSH WHOOSH! Benny and pillow rest under the moon!
