# Sharing Picnic Basket

## Topic
- Hook: Benny learns sharing snacks makes the picnic happier
- Age: 3-8
- Category: moral
- Moral: Sharing is caring
- Visual: Pastel meadow picnic, friendly squirrel and duck

## Script
Benny brought a picnic basket with three berry muffins.
He saw Sia Squirrel looking hungry near the blanket.
Then Dodo Duck waddled over with a rumbly tummy.
Benny looked at the muffins and thought for a moment.
I can share, he said with a big warm smile.
One muffin for Sia, one for Dodo, and one for Benny.
The picnic felt bigger when everyone was happy.
Benny learned that sharing is caring.
