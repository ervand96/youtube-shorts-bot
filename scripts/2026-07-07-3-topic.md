# Color Counting Balloons

## Topic
- Hook: Red, blue, yellow, green balloons help Benny count to five
- Age: 2-8
- Category: learning
- Moral: Learning is fun when we say it together
- Visual: Sunny park, colorful balloons, playful counting numbers

## Script
Benny found five balloons in the sunny park.
One red balloon bobbed up high.
Two blue balloons danced side to side.
Three yellow balloons made a bright hello.
Four green balloons floated nice and slow.
Five rainbow balloons made Benny cheer.
One, two, three, four, five. Great counting, friend!
Colors and numbers are fun to learn together.
