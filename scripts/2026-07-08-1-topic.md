# Good Morning Song for Kids

## Topic
- Hook: Benny wakes up the sunny room with a clap-clap good morning beat
- Age: 2-8
- Category: nursery
- Moral: A cheerful routine helps friends start the day kindly
- Visual: Benny, sunbeams, toothbrush, cereal bowl, dancing slippers

## Script
CLAP CLAP! Benny jumps up and sings GOOD MORNING!
The sun peeks in with a warm yellow light.
Brush, brush, brush, little teeth shine bright.
Stomp, stomp, stomp, two happy feet.
Benny waves hello to every friend he meets.
Good morning, good morning, let's smile and play.
Kind little helpers start a brand new day.
CLAP CLAP! Sing bye-bye until the next sunny song!
