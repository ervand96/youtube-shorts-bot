# Wheels on the Bus Song

## Topic
- Hook: Benny drives a friendly yellow bus with beep-beep wheels and waving animal friends
- Age: 2-8
- Category: nursery
- Moral: Singing together makes every ride happy and safe
- Visual: Benny, yellow bus, smiling toddlers, waving puppy, sunny road, rainbow stop sign

## Script
BEEP BEEP! The bus goes ROUND and ROUND!
Hop on with Benny, up through town.
The doors go open with a swish-swish sound.
The wipers go wiggle when raindrops come down.
Wave hello, then clap to the beat.
Round and round on the sunny street.
BEEP BEEP! Benny's bus says see you soon!
