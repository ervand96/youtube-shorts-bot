# Baby Shark Dance Song for Kids

## Topic
- Hook: Benny splashes into a safe pretend ocean with a clap-clap shark dance
- Age: 2-8
- Category: nursery
- Moral: Dancing together is more fun when everyone gets a turn
- Visual: Benny, smiling baby shark, blue waves, bubbles, dancing starfish

## Script
CLAP CLAP! Baby shark splashes UP and DOWN!
Benny wiggles fins all around the town.
Mama shark smiles with a swish, swish, sway.
Papa shark claps to the happy play.
Starfish spin while bubbles pop bright.
Everyone takes turns and dances just right.
CLAP CLAP! Wave bye-bye, little shark friends!
