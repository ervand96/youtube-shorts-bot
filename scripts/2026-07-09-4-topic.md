# Please and Thank You Story for Kids

## Topic
- Hook: Benny hears a toy train toot and learns polite words with friends
- Age: 2-8
- Category: moral
- Moral: Saying please and thank you helps friends feel cared for
- Visual: Toy train, snack table, Benny, puppy friend, thank-you cards

## Script
TOOT TOOT! The toy train rolls FAST to Benny!
A puppy asks, please pass the red car to me.
Benny smiles and gives it a gentle push.
Thank you, says Puppy, in a happy hush.
Please makes asking kind and clear.
Thank you tells a friend, I am glad you are here.
TOOT TOOT! Polite words make playtime shine!
