# Benny Bus Rainbow Song

## Topic
- Hook: The wheels on Benny bus spin through a rainbow town
- Age: 2-8
- Category: nursery
- Moral: Singing together makes every ride cheerful
- Visual: Bright cartoon bus, rainbow road, smiling clouds

## Script
The wheels on Benny bus go round and round.
Round and round through Rainbow Town.
The doors on Benny bus go open and shut.
Open and shut for friends to hop up.
The wipers on Benny bus go swish, swish, swish.
Swish, swish, swish past every wish.
Benny sings, red, blue, yellow, green.
A happy little ride in a shiny bus scene.
Wave bye-bye, friends. See you on the next song!
