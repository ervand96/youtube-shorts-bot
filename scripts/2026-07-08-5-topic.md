# Old MacDonald Farm Animals Song

## Topic
- Hook: Benny visits a sunny farm and sings animal sounds with friends
- Age: 2-8
- Category: animals
- Moral: Listening carefully helps us learn each animal sound
- Visual: Barn, cow, pig, sheep, duck, Benny with a red farm hat

## Script
MOO MOO! Benny opens the big red barn door!
Old MacDonald smiles and taps one, two.
The happy cow says moo, moo, moo.
The pink little pig says oink, oink, hey.
The fluffy sheep says baa all day.
The duck goes quack by the pond so blue.
Benny sings, farm friends, we love you.
MOO MOO! Clap for every animal sound!
