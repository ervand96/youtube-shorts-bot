# Happy Farm Animal Band

## Topic
- Hook: The cow says moo and the duck says quack in Benny farm band
- Age: 2-8
- Category: animals
- Moral: Every voice can join the song
- Visual: Colorful farm, barn stage, cute cow, duck, sheep, pig

## Script
Welcome to Benny farm animal band!
The cow taps the drum and says moo, moo, moo.
The duck shakes bells and says quack, quack, quack.
The sheep strums softly and says baa, baa, baa.
The pig claps along and says oink, oink, oink.
Benny waves his paws and sings la, la, la.
Moo, quack, baa, oink, la. What a happy farm song!
Every animal voice makes the music bright.
