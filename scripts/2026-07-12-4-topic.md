# Kind Words Story for Kids

## Topic
- Hook: Benny hears a tumble-bump block tower fall and uses kind words to help a friend
- Age: 2-8
- Category: moral
- Moral: Kind words can turn a mistake into teamwork
- Visual: Benny, friend Mia, colorful blocks, picnic blanket, heart balloons, sunny meadow

## Script
TUMBLE BUMP! The block tower falls down!
Mia frowns and looks at the ground.
Benny says, it's okay, let's try once more.
Kind words open a happy door.
They stack red, blue, yellow, green.
Teamwork makes the tallest tower seen.
TUMBLE BUMP! Now everyone cheers with kind words!
