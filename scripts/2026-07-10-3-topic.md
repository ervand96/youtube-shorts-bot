# Learn Colors for Toddlers with Benny

## Topic
- Hook: Benny pops colorful bubbles and names red, yellow, blue, and green
- Age: 2-8
- Category: learning
- Moral: We can learn by looking, naming, and trying again
- Visual: Color bubbles, Benny paint brush, red apple, yellow duck, blue ball, green frog

## Script
POP POP! Benny finds colors in the air!
Red is the apple, shiny and round.
Yellow is the duck with a quack-quack sound.
Blue is the ball that bounces high.
Green is the frog jumping by.
Point to a color, say it with me.
Red, yellow, blue, green, happy as can be.
POP POP! Great job learning colors today!
