# Sleepy Star Pillow

## Topic
- Hook: A sleepy star needs help finding its soft cloud pillow
- Age: 2-8
- Category: bedtime
- Moral: Gentle bedtime routines help everyone rest
- Visual: Cozy night sky, soft moon, tiny glowing star

## Script
Once upon a time, a tiny star yawned in the sky.
It could not find its soft cloud pillow.
Benny Bunny looked up and whispered, I can help.
He asked the moon to shine a gentle silver path.
The little star followed the path to a fluffy cloud.
It brushed its sparkles, took one slow breath, and snuggled in.
Benny smiled from his window below.
Good night, little star. Good night, sleepy friends.
