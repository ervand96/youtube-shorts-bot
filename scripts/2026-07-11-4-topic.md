# Kind Crayon Sharing Story

## Topic
- Hook: Benny hears a snap-snap crayon box and learns to share colors
- Age: 2-8
- Category: moral
- Moral: Sharing helps everyone make something beautiful
- Visual: Art table, big paper sun, crayons, two friends, rainbow drawing

## Script
SNAP SNAP! Benny opens the bright crayon box!
He grabs red, blue, yellow, and green.
Mia wants one color for her sunny scene.
Benny pauses and looks around.
One little crayon can make two smiles found.
They share the colors and draw a rainbow heart.
SNAP SNAP! Kind friends make the best art!
