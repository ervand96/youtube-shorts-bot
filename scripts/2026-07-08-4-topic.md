# Kindness Story for Kids

## Topic
- Hook: Benny hears a wobbling wagon and helps a friend with kindness
- Age: 2-8
- Category: moral
- Moral: Kind helpers use gentle words and take turns
- Visual: Toy wagon, blocks, Benny, squirrel friend, sunny playroom

## Script
RATTLE RATTLE! Benny spots a wagon wobbling fast!
A little squirrel frowns beside spilled blocks.
Benny says, I can help, then gently stops.
He picks up red, then blue, then green.
Together they make the tidiest scene.
The squirrel says, thank you for being kind.
Benny smiles, helping friends feels fine.
RATTLE RATTLE! Kind hearts make playtime bright!
