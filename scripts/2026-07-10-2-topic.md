# Cloud Train Bedtime Story for Kids

## Topic
- Hook: Benny hears a soft choo-choo cloud train that carries sleepy friends to dreamland
- Age: 2-8
- Category: bedtime
- Moral: Gentle breathing and a calm bedtime routine help little ones rest
- Visual: Cozy bedroom, cloud train, teddy bear, blanket, moon window

## Script
CHOO CHOO! The cloud train floats soft and slow!
Benny tucks Teddy under a warm little sheet.
One small breath goes in, then out, so sweet.
The moon waves hello through the window light.
Cloud wheels whisper hush-hush tonight.
Benny says, close your eyes, you are safe and loved.
Dream stars twinkle gently above.
CHOO CHOO! The cozy train says good night!
