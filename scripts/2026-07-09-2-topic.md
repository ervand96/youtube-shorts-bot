# Sleepy Firefly Bedtime Story for Kids

## Topic
- Hook: Benny follows a glowing firefly through a quiet bedtime garden
- Age: 2-8
- Category: bedtime
- Moral: Calm breathing and gentle light help little friends rest
- Visual: Benny, tiny firefly, moonlit flowers, cozy blanket, soft garden path

## Script
GLOW GLOW! The firefly twinkles SOFT and SLOW!
Benny tiptoes where the night flowers grow.
One deep breath makes the garden still.
Two sleepy eyes rest by the hill.
The firefly blinks a tiny good night.
Benny tucks in the blanket just right.
GLOW GLOW! Sweet dreams until morning light!
