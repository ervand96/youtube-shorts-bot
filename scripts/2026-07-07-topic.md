# The Brave Little Bunny

## Topic
- Hook: A tiny bunny learns to be brave
- Age: 3-8
- Moral: Courage grows when we try
- Visual: Pastel meadow, cute cartoon bunny

## Script
Meet Benny the bunny. He was scared of the big garden gate.
Every morning he watched other animals play outside.
One day, Benny took one tiny hop closer.
Then another hop. And another!
The gate was not scary at all. It was full of friends and sunshine.
Benny smiled. Being brave means trying, even when you feel small.
