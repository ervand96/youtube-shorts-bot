# Sharing Lesson Story for Kids

## Topic
- Hook: Benny hears a cookie plate wiggle and learns to share one treat at a time
- Age: 2-8
- Category: moral
- Moral: Sharing and saying thank you helps friends feel included
- Visual: Cookie plate, Benny, fox friend, bear friend, round table, napkins

## Script
CRUNCH CRUNCH! The cookie plate wiggles on the table!
Benny sees one cookie, then two friends smile.
He breaks it in pieces and waits a little while.
One for Fox, one for Bear, one for me.
Thank you, friends, we share happily.
The table feels warm when everyone is kind.
Benny learns sharing brings smiles to find.
CRUNCH CRUNCH! Kind friends share the sweetest treat!
