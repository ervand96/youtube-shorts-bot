# Moon Pillow Bedtime Story for Kids

## Topic
- Hook: Benny hears a hush-hush moonbeam and helps a pillow find sleep
- Age: 2-8
- Category: bedtime
- Moral: Gentle routines help little friends rest
- Visual: Cozy bedroom, moon pillow, star lamp, soft blanket, sleepy teddy bear

## Script
HUSH HUSH! Benny tiptoes under twinkle stars!
Moon Pillow wiggles and cannot rest.
Benny pats the blanket, soft and best.
One deep breath, then two slow yawns.
The stars hum softly until worry is gone.
Moon Pillow smiles with sleepy light.
Benny whispers, good night, sleep tight.
