# Learn Numbers and Shapes for Toddlers

## Topic
- Hook: Benny pops bright shape bubbles and counts one, two, three
- Age: 2-8
- Category: learning
- Moral: Learning feels easy when we count slowly and try again
- Visual: Benny, red circle, blue square, yellow triangle, counting garden, bouncing bubbles

## Script
POP POP! Benny counts SHAPES one, two, three!
One red circle rolls by me.
Two blue squares jump up and down.
Three yellow triangles spin around.
Point, count, clap, and say hooray.
Shapes and numbers are friends today.
POP POP! Great counting, little learner!
